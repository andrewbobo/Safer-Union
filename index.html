About R & H Manufacturing, LLC

R & H Machine is a manufacturing company located in Longview, Texas producing intricate parts to solve issues in the oil & gas industry. With more than 25 years of experience, a highly qualified team, and a stringent API Q1 compliant QMS in place, the quality of products is insured at every production stage.

Capabilities:

28,880 sq. ft. climate controlled machine shop

10,000 sq. ft. fabrication facility.

Quality

On February 1,1992, R&H Machine was open for business. If you think prayers don't work, I started R&H Machine with only $3200.00 dollars to my name. It was tough, I was the only employee and a single dad, but this is where the workaholic takes over.

I never ran from a challenge. Somehow I would always figure out how to take of it. R&H Machine started growing almost as quickly as we had opened the doors.

In November of 2006 we moved into our current location with 26,880 sq ft. As you can see we are not afraid of success. We put taking care of our customer first every time. We have both manual and CNC machines as well as fabrication capabilities.

We have approximately 45 employees to take on whatever the world has to offer. We have totally been blessed and we seek out the challenges.
